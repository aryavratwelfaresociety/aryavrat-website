About the Organisation

Aryavrat Welfare Society is a non-profit organization focused on sustainable development through traditional Indian values, water conservation, education, health drives, and promoting the Indian Knowledge System (IKS).

About Us

Mission: To empower communities by promoting sustainable practices rooted in Indian traditions.

Vision: To create a culturally enriched and environmentally conscious society.

Values: Transparency, Compassion, Empowerment, Sustainability.
