About the Organisation

Aryavrat Welfare Society is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to promoting sustainable development through the revival of traditional Indian values and practices. The society actively works in areas such as water conservation, promotion of the Indian Knowledge System (IKS), and the organization of national and international seminars and conferences on a wide range of social and cultural issues.

A key focus is water conservationâ€”reviving ancient water management techniques like stepwells, rainwater harvesting, and community-based solutions. Through awareness drives, workshops, and field programs, it educates people about water sustainability.

It also supports the Indian Knowledge System, collaborating with experts to preserve traditions like Ayurveda, classical learning, and Vedic science.

We host impactful seminars, webinars, and conferences on issues like education, health, environment, women empowerment, and culture to create meaningful change.
